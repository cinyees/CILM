Personal Sentiment
Upon hearing that the maximum word count was 1000, it is tempting to sit down for one session and write an essay for the last time. However, I knew that writing an essay to express my reflections would not do the complexity of my experience with the module justice. I knew that I needed to create something that was as difficult to progress through as the module was for me, and I wanted to evoke the same feelings by making the marker go through the same choices. This could not be done by simply expressing my own thoughts in writing - whilst empathy of the reader may enable them to understand to an extent, I did not think that this would make the same impact. Further, the summation of the module as an essay would not reflect the abstractness of the module.
