Welcome to CILM

Before proceeding with any path for this module, please read the instructions:


    1. You will be presented with a task, and a choice of how you approach the task.
    2. You are free to choose your approach. Bear in mind, as the title of the game page suggests, you get out what you make of it.
    3. Try to make your choice quickly and instinctively: real life offers no time to logically evaluate every decision you make.
    4. Once you reach an ending, you cannot return to the last option. You must begin the game again, and remake all your decisions.
    5. In case of exhaustion, a cheat sheet has been uploaded to WBS.
    6. Now that you have read all the instructions, please click the mirror image to begin your path.
